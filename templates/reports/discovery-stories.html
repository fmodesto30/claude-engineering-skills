3. Stories

E1US-01 · Generate an Invoice from an Order

As a billing operator, I want an Invoice generated automatically when an Order is confirmed, so that the Customer is billed without manual entry.

Acceptance criteria
  • Given a confirmed Order, when generation runs, then one Invoice with matching LineItems exists.
  • Money totals on the Invoice equal the sum of LineItem amounts plus applicable TaxRule.
  • Re-running generation for the same Order does not create a duplicate Invoice.

E2US-02 · Reconcile a Payment against an Invoice

As a finance analyst, I want incoming Payments matched to open Invoices, so that outstanding balances stay accurate.

Acceptance criteria
  • A Payment equal to the Invoice total marks the Invoice as settled.
  • A partial Payment reduces the balance and keeps the Invoice open.
  • Every match writes an AuditEntry.
